Topic wrap-up and reflection

container

In this topic you have learnt to:

  1. Understand the notion of responsible AI
  2. Understand why we need Responsible AI
  3. Recognise basic principles of Responsible AI
  4. Understand current research and discussion of Responsible AI

The automation of decision making is now commonplace and has implications for all of us. When people make decisions, the social and historical context is an influence along with rationality and human factors. Algorithmic systems base their decisions on data and computation. The impacts of these systems will be examined. Are you aware of all the algorithmic systems that make decisions in your day-to-day life?
