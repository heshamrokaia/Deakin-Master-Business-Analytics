Benefits of Automation

Using automation can assist businesses in maintaining profitability by increasing output, reducing errors, and saving time and resources. Automation is a cost-saving tool that can also increase productivity and profits during these difficult economic times. There are many good arguments to be made that how technology is used and deployed that creates good outcomes for individuals, companies, and society. PwC estimates that AI could contribute up to $15.7 trillion to the global economy in 2030 (Kay Firth-Butterfield, 2021).
