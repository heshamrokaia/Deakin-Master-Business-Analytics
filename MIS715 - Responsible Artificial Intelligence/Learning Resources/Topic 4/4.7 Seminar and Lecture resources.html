Seminar and Lecture resources

Preparation for the seminar: 

  • Read Assignment 1 Hints
  • Read the Topic 4 sections and complete activities.
  • Take notes to help you learn and organise information
  • Read the following papers:
    • Mayer, A.S., Strich, F. and Fiedler, M., 2020. Unintended Consequences of Introducing AI Systems for Decision Making. MIS Quarterly Executive, 19(4). 
    • Candelon, F., di Carlo, R. C., De Bondt, M., & Evgeniou, T. (2021). AI Regulation is Coming. Harvard Business Review, 99(5).
  • Note down any questions to bring up during the class

In Seminar Tasks

After reading the above papers:

Task 1. Discuss with your peers and answer the following questions:

  1. What are the unintended consequences of introducing a decision-making AI system?
  2. How can organizations manage these unintended consequences?
  3. Explain how a workflow process for dataset gathering, adaptation, and documentation (i.e., Datasheets for Datasets) can help reduce unintended consequences and improve AI transparency and accountability? 

Task 2. Each group will present their answers to the class (or add your answers to discussion forum)
