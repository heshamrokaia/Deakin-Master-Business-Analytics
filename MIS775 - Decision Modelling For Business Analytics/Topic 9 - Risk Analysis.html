Introduction

  • The best way to evaluate risk is to simulate the range of possible outcomes of a business decision and analyse and quantify the risks.

  • Using past behaviour it is possible to capture the range and likelihood of a random variable with a probability distribution.

  • This topic focuses on the use of simulation to build and use spreadsheet-based stochastic decision models to carry out quantitative risk analysis.

  

Learning Objectives/Outcomes

  • Using empirical distributions to generate stochastic input data

  • Capturing probabilistic outcomes of multiple random inputs using simulation

  • Simulation output analysis

  • Model accuracy and limitations of simulation
