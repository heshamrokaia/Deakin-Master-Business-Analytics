Introduction

This topic focuses on problem structuring and encourages students to use a framework for building spreadsheet based decision models so that complex business decisions can be modelled and analysed effectively and in a logical manner.

Learning Objectives/Outcomes

  • Problem structuring for stochastic decision models
  • Using a framework for building spreadsheet-based decision models
  • Testing and validating a spreadsheet model
  • Performing scenario analysis.

Textbook reading

No textbook reading for this topic. However, we draw heavily upon Chapter 16 of Quantitative Methods for Business by Anderson et. al., available under Week 7 Learning Resources
