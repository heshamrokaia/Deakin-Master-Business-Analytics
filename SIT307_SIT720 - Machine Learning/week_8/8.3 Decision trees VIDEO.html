Decision trees

A decision tree is a map of the possible outcomes of a series of related choices.

Decision trees can be used to weigh possible actions against one another based on their costs, benefits and probabilities.

A decision tree typically starts with a single root node, which branches into possible outcomes. In the video you will see an example of how a decision tree can be used to predict commute time.
