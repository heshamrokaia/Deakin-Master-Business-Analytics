Hacking is an increasing threat to the privacy of individuals, companies and nations.

The danger of cyber attack

In 2018, hotel chain Marriott discovered unauthorised access to its customer data. The data contained both encrypted and clear text passport numbers, names, dates of birth, and addresses.

The company found that up to 300 million accounts were affected, but was unable to determine the exact number due to duplicate records in the database.

Following the breach, Marriott took the following steps:

  • Informed customers of the breach
  • Set up hotlines and a website for customers to find information
  • Provided free identity monitoring services to impacted guests
  • Established a fraud claim process for guests whose unencrypted passport number was used for fraudulent purposes
