eVALUate

Thank you for participating in this unit, SIT720 Machine Learning. We hope you enjoyed the course and found these learning materials stimulating and engaging.

Deakin University uses a range of tools to understand the students’ learning experience. We use this information to make the unit better for the next students.

The student evaluation survey (eVALUate) plays a central role in this. The survey is completely anonymous and has just 13 questions (11 quantitative and 2 qualitative).

Activity

Think about the following questions:

  • What did you like about this unit?
  • What could we do better?
  • Was there more that you wanted to know?

We’d appreciate it if you could take a few minutes to let us know what you thought about this unit by completing the short eVALUate survey.

Please note:, Generally the survey opens towards the end of week 10 or beginning of week 11, and remains open for a month or so before it closes. Check the home page for announcements or keep an eye out in the discussion forum postings. You can also contact your unit chair if in doubt.

Thank you again for all your comments and participation.
