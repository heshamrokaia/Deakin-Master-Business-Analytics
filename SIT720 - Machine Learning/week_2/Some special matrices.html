Some special matrices

The representation of a set of documents as vectors is known as the vector space model.

It is a fundamental step in information retrieval operations. These operations can vary from a simple query problem to real search engines.

In the text data representation step we had two documents with their corresponding feature vectors.

By calculating the euclidean distance, we can easily find the distance between these two documents. It can be utilised as a quick way to find the closeness of two documents.
